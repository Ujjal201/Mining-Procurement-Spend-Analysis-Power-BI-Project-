# ⛏️ Mining Procurement Spend Analysis (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes procurement spending, cost structure, and profitability across multiple mining sites. The goal is to identify cost drivers, improve efficiency, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Mining companies often struggle with:
- High procurement costs
- Lack of visibility in cost distribution
- Inefficient resource allocation

This dashboard solves these challenges by providing clear insights into revenue, cost, and profit drivers.

---

## 📈 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue: $11.0bn
- Total Cost: $7.19bn
- Profit: $3.82bn
- Profit Margin: 34.7%
- Cost per Ton: 318.3

## 💡 Key Insights
- Beta Mine has the highest profit margin (~44.5%)
- Fuel and labor are the biggest cost drivers
- Central region contributes the highest cost share
- Profitability varies significantly across mines
